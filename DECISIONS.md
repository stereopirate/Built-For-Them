### 🟡 Adjustable Activity Table (BFT-002) — IN PROGRESS

**What it does:** Accessible activity surface for children using wheelchairs, feeder seats, or adaptive positioning equipment. Open-front design clears adaptive seating. Interchangeable tops for daily use, creative play, and sensory/LEGO activity.

**Commercial equivalent:** Tumble Forms 2 Feeder Seat Tray — ~$200+. Ours: est. $60–90 in materials.

**V1 scope:** Freestanding table, basic height adjustment, three interchangeable tops sharing one body cutout. Modular accessories deferred to v2.

**Status:** Concepting — top retention mechanism and body cutout dimensions are the current blocker.
