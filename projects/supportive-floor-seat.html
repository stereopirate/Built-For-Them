Seating

Supportive Floor Seat

A configurable low floor seat concept intended to support supervised play while allowing future adjustment of lateral and trunk supports.

Early idea

Project overview

This project is intentionally not presented as a build plan yet. Seating and positioning involve significant fit, pressure, fall, and restraint risks. The first phase is requirements gathering with families and qualified therapists.

Development status: Early idea

This page documents an in-progress idea. It is not a released or clinically validated plan.

Design goals

  • Affordable and made from commonly available components
  • Clear enough for a careful home builder or community maker
  • Easy to inspect, maintain, and repair
  • Adaptable without hiding important limitations

Next steps

  • Define the specific activity and support needs
  • Review existing commercial and open-source options
  • Establish a safe adjustment and release strategy
  • Decide whether this concept should proceed
