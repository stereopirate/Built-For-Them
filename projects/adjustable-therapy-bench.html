Positioning

Adjustable Therapy Bench

A compact 3/4-inch plywood therapy bench with four height positions and independent front/rear adjustment for controlled tilt.

Prototype

Project overview

The current small-bench concept uses a 24 × 10 inch top, inner legs, and trapezoid outer legs with vertical channels and horizontal adjustment slots. It is intended for supervised positioning and therapy activities after professional review.

Development status: Prototype

This page documents an in-progress idea. It is not a released or clinically validated plan.

Design goals

  • Affordable and made from commonly available components
  • Clear enough for a careful home builder or community maker
  • Easy to inspect, maintain, and repair
  • Adaptable without hiding important limitations

Next steps

  • Build and inspect the revised prototype
  • Verify slot geometry and hardware clearances
  • Round all exposed edges and seal surfaces
  • Document load testing and therapist feedback
