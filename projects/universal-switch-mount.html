Switch access

Universal Switch Mount

A modular mounting system intended to connect common assistive switches with standard camera-mount hardware, clamps, plates, and multiple surfaces.

Concept

Project overview

The core idea is to avoid a one-purpose mount. A base adapter would hold the switch, while a standard 1/4-20 connection opens access to inexpensive camera arms, clamps, tripods, suction mounts, and wheelchair-compatible mounting hardware.

Development status: Concept

This page documents an in-progress idea. It is not a released or clinically validated plan.

Design goals

  • Affordable and made from commonly available components
  • Clear enough for a careful home builder or community maker
  • Easy to inspect, maintain, and repair
  • Adaptable without hiding important limitations

Next steps

  • Confirm switch sizes and cable-clearance needs
  • Prototype interchangeable switch cradles
  • Test anti-rotation features around the 1/4-20 connection
  • Evaluate clamp, tabletop, suction, and soft-surface bases
