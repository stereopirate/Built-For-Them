Adjustable Therapy Bench

A compact plywood bench with independent front and rear adjustment for height and tilt.

Top size24 × 10 inches
Lowest heightAbout 7 inches
Material3/4-inch plywood
Adjustment4 positions

What this project does

The bench uses inner and outer leg assemblies. Bolts pass through vertical channels and lock into horizontal slots, allowing the front and rear of the top to be raised independently.

Safety and fit: This is a general DIY project. Confirm dimensions, positioning, hardware, supervision, and suitability with the child's care team before use. Inspect the bench before every session.

Core dimensions

PartDimensions
Bench top24 in L × 10 in W × 3/4 in
Inner legs10 in W × 7 in H
Outer legs7 in H trapezoid; 10 in top, 14 in bottom
Overall lowest size24 in W × 7 in H × 10 in D

Recommended tools

  • Circular saw or track saw
  • Jigsaw
  • Drill/driver
  • Router or sanding tools for rounded edges
  • Clamps, square, measuring tools, and safety equipment

Before you build

  1. Read the complete PDF guide before cutting.
  2. Confirm the finished dimensions suit the intended child and activity.
  3. Use quality plywood with smooth, undamaged faces.
  4. Round and sand all exposed edges.
  5. Use locking hardware that cannot loosen during use.
