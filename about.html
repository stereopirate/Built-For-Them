About Built For Them

Better access through practical making.

Built For Them is an early-stage project focused on lowering the barriers between a family’s need and a useful piece of adaptive equipment.

Why this exists

Families often discover that a simple positioning aid, mount, step, seat, or therapy tool is expensive, unavailable, or difficult to customize. At the same time, many woodworkers, designers, 3D-printer owners, and community makers want to help but lack clear plans.

Built For Them aims to bridge that gap with transparent project documentation, realistic build requirements, and a strong emphasis on professional review and safe use.

What this is not

  • Not a medical provider
  • Not a substitute for professional evaluation
  • Not a promise that every idea is appropriate for every child
  • Not yet a marketplace or maker-matching service
