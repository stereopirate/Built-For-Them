Why this exists

It started with one build. It won't stop there.

Build with care. Share with love.

My son has Cerebral Palsy. When his therapist recommended a height-adjustable therapy bench for home practice, I looked up the commercial version: $300-400, often not covered by insurance. So I built one. It took a weekend, a single sheet of Baltic birch plywood, and about $50 in materials.

It worked just as well. His therapist approved it. And I thought - every family should be able to do this.

Built For Them is where I share what I've made, with full plans, cut lists, and the kind of detail you actually need to replicate it at home. No experience required beyond basic tools and a few hours. The therapy bench is the project that got this started - but it's the first one, not the only one. There's more I hope to design and share over time.
